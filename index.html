I was born on July 19, 2005 in Anaheim, California but I was raised in San Bernardino my whole life. I come from two immigrant parents so I am a first generation. Growing up I have seen the struggles my parents have underwent through in order to provide for my siblings and I. Taking low wage jobs with little to no benfits in order for us to have a better future with oppurtunities.

Seeing the struggles first hand not only in my family but also in my community as well, I knew I had to take action. I wanted to make something of myself so my parents' hard work would not go to waste. I decided I wanted to become an Immigration Lawyer to help my community that is also facing the same struggle my family and I had. So being an Immigarion Lawyer is what I plan to do.

I am currently enrolled as an Undergraduate studying a political Sceince Major at the University of California Riverside. I am working towards a minor in Philosphy and I am expected to graduate 2027. In which I will continue my journey to law school to fulfill the requirments needed for my dream job.
